Eight Capacities

Integrity

Consistent regulation of behavior that aligns actions with principles, guided by a genuine commitment to doing what is right.

Sense-making

Converts data into structured schemas by integrating metacognitive signals, addressing allocentric perspectives, reconciling discrepancies across inputs, and revising preconceptions.

Critical Thinking

Defermines the prior plausibility of assertions, checks for source reliability, distinguishes between claims, evidence, and inference as appropriate, evaluates the plausibility at each stage of reasoning, and evaluates logical validity and evidential relevance.

Objectivity

Mitigates motivated reasoning by identifying uneven evidentiary standards, then uniformly applying empirical criteria and bayes-updating to all conclusions (through consistent rules and self-critique).

Fairness

Maintains impartial balance by using an objective input–outcome ratio to weigh empathy (in terms of intent), against accountability (in terms of consequence).

Confidence

Self-appraisal of past achievements, views setbacks as temporary skill gaps and aligns self-belief with demonstrated performance.

Intentionality

Allocate effort according to future importance rather than sunk costs, minimize unnecessary decision load or temptation exposure, and preserve resources for highest priorities.

Perseverance

Long-term persistence of action, utilizes gradual exposure and skill-development, overriding fear-driven avoidance.
