Key Capacities

Integrity

Consistent regulation of behavior that aligns actions with principles, guided by a commitment to doing what is right.

Sense-making

Organizes data into structured schemas by integrating metacognitive signals, and addressing allocentric perspectives.

Critical Thinking

Defermines the plausibility of assertions; checks for source credibility; distinguishes the fine line between claims, evidence, and inference; and stress-tests for evidential relevance.

Objectivity

Mitigates motivated reasoning by identifying inconsistent evidentiary standards, then uniformly applies empirical criteria to all conclusions.

Fairness

Balances between the intent behind a person's choices (or their level of altruism), with the consequences of those actions.

Internal Locus of Control

Allocate effort according to future leverage rather than sunk costs, to minimize decision overload.

Perseverance

Long-term persistence of action. It utilizes gradual exposure to override fear-driven avoidance.
